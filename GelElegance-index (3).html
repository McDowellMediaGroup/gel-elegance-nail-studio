Meet Your Artist

Crafted with
Intention

Welcome to Gel Elegance Nail Studio — a luxury nail experience designed for women who appreciate the finer details. Every set is hand-crafted with precision, premium products, and a deep passion for nail artistry.

With over 32 years of experience in the nail industry, I have dedicated my career to the art and science of beautiful nails. What started as a passion became a lifelong craft — and today, every client who sits across from me receives the benefit of three decades of skill, precision, and genuine care. I specialize in gel manicures, builder gel, and luxurious pedicure services, using only premium products to ensure results that are stunning, long-lasting, and gentle on your natural nails. My philosophy is simple: every detail matters, every client deserves to feel pampered, and no two sets of nails should look exactly the same. Whether you're coming in for your weekly maintenance or treating yourself to something special, you'll always leave feeling polished, confident, and beautiful. I can't wait to meet you.
